Most Popular Unit:

Square Meter:

Who: The concept of the square meter, as a unit of area measurement, is not attributed to a specific individual. It is part of the International System of Units (SI), which is a globally recognized and standardized system for measurement.

How: The square meter is derived from the meter, which is the standard unit for length in the SI system. A square meter represents the area of a square with sides that are each one meter long.

When: The SI system, which includes the square meter as the standard unit for area, was officially established in 1960. It was developed by the General Conference on Weights and Measures (CGPM) and the International System of Units (SI) to provide a consistent and standardized system of measurement worldwide.

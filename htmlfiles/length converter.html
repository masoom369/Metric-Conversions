Length

Length: The idea of length measurement has a long history, with various cultures developing their own systems. The concept of the inch, foot, and yard can be traced back to ancient civilizations. The modern standardization of length units began in the 18th century when the French Academy of Sciences proposed the metric system. The meter was introduced as the standard unit of length in 1795 during the French Revolution and later adopted internationally, revolutionizing length measurement.
